What we do

Research

Collaborate directly with MDM to guarantee privacy to the brainstorming of your initial prototype.

Design

Rely upon MDM designers to create an exclusive and efficient mechanical device.

Development

Check step-by-step the professional work of MDM until the production launch of your components.

We design machines, applications and prototypes for packaging industries. (filling, capping and labeling machines).

We can offer:

  • feasibility study;
  • costs analysis;
  • 3D model;
  • calculation reports and technical analysis;
  • executive drawings;
  • coordination of suppliers for the construction of working prototypes.

Packaging & Labelling

After a decades-long activity of design and production of filling machines, capping and labeling machines, MDM has gained experience in these areas bringing innovative ideas in many other fields concerning the mechanics.
