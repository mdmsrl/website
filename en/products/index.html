For Bikes

Trype

Trype is a support for the maintenance of mountain-bikes (MTB) on the field. Its versability allows you to place the bike in different ways, depending on the type of maintenance work to be executed.

Learn more on trype.it

No more damages to your bike

With TRYPE the bike is leaning against the fork tubes and the hub of the pedals. Contrary to most commercial supports, the bike is kept in place by a sturdy clamp that is tightened on the members of the frame or on the seat tube. In fact, the gripper often generates concentrated forces that may damage the paint, or even worse, the thin shells made of carbon fiber. With TRYPE these problems were overcome.

Hopper Stand

Hopper Stand is a practical support that is suitable on the field for parking quickly bicycles of any type and size. The bicycles are hung on a horizontal pole for the extreminity or the back of the saddle.

Find out more on trype.it

Increasingly stable and light

Hopper Stand is very popular among cycling teams of Triathlon: it is made with tubes of generous section (anodized aluminum), being more stable, rigid and lighter compared to the traditional solutions with iron pipes. It is easily dismantled and can be put away in its bag.

 

Bike Rack

Bike Rack is a modular system applicable to small and medium trailer-trucks. Its effectiveness allows you to carry up to 8 bicycles in a length of two meters, ensuring a total security of cargo securing and low center of gravity.

Find out more on trype.it

Security first

Bike Rack is suitable for all types of bicycle (26”- 28”- 29”) both MTB and road bikes. The bicycles are securely fastened to the Bike Rack using the attack of the front wheel. The bikes have no contact with each other, and therefore, they do not get damaged during the transport.

Unlike other systems on the market, with Bike Rack the truck bed is walkable and can be used to transport other things. It can be removed and disassembled from the truck in few minutes and you can put it back vertically in the garage corner.

Machines

Bordix

Bordix is a small counter machine for the closure of automotive instruments, such as speedometer and tachometer. Once the instrument is open to be fixed, Bordix optimizes the time and performs the edge of the ring containment to perfection.

Your tool as new

Bordix ensures the integrity of the instrument at the end of the processing. Precisely for this reason, it is particularly appreciated by restorers of vintage cars that find their tools as closed as they were originally. Moreover, Bordix comes with detailed instructions for use and CE marking.

 

Filling Valves

MDM designs and creates small lots of special valves for weight filling machine and flowometer machines. Depending on your needs, MDM collaborate with the manufacturer of the machine and/or with the user at a convenine price.

Say goodbye to dirt

On some filling machine or flowometer machines we have eliminated the problem of cleaning the small net of taps, installing melter valves without small nets (we have designed and created them).

 

Stirling

Stirling is an external combustion engine able to turn around 1000 rpm without the need for re-lubrication, developing a power of about 600 W. In the coming months we will continue the test of the sealing rings of the pistons in different materials.

A cutting-edge engine

Sitlring is an engine developed by MDM after extensive research in contemporary literature and technical reports by the NASA. The aim was to make an industrial type of engine, different from various educational models that can be found in trade. MDM is looking for a partner to develop a pellet fuelled electrical generator.
